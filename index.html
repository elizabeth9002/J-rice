Jollof Rice Recipe

ingredients

  • Rice
  • pepper
  • onions
  • Seasoning
  • salt, et.c

procedures for preparing jollof rice

  1. Cook the rice till tender and set aside.
  2. Pour chicken stock into the pot.
  3. Add the tomato stew, the coocked rice,
    check that the water is the same level as the rice.
  4. Add pepper, salt & seasoning to taste.
  5. Stir, cook at low medium heat till the water is dry.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
